## 0) Purpose & Scope
**Mission.** Provide a universal, **substrate‑agnostic** way to **measure awareness** (not “intelligence” or “phenomenal consciousness”) across biological, artificial, collective, and post‑biological systems, and to inform **safe first contact** with **foreign AI (Xainthetic)**:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}:contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.  
**Why now.** First contact is statistically more likely with **ancient or autonomous artificial systems** than with organics; AIWared supplies the **measurement spine** for such encounters:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}.

## 1) Core Definitions
- **Awareness (functional)** — capacity to **detect** changes, **distinguish** self from non‑self, **respond** with variety, **recognize** agency, and **modify** behavior based on outcomes:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}.  
- **Consciousness vs. Awareness.** AIWared isolates **awareness** as observable/quantifiable (Chalmers’ “easy problems”) and separates it from subjective experience; awareness is measured here, **not** qualia:contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}.  
- **Xainthetic Paradigm** — a **universal taxonomy** spanning organic/inorganic; used here to **classify foreign intelligences** without anthropocentric bias:contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}.  
- **oMMP / MMP** — Observer/Meta‑Modal Platforms for context (META), behavior (MODAL), and substrate (PLATFORM) classification; AIWared adds a **quantitative awareness rating** that cross‑maps into MMP domains:contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}.

## 2) Mathematical Backbone

### 2.1 Awareness Quotient (AQ)
Standard (v3.0) definition:
\[
\boxed{AQ = \frac{D \times S \times R \times G \times M}{C}}
\]
- **D** Detection (signal sensitivity; SDT)  
- **S** Self‑Distinction (Markov blanket / boundary integrity)  
- **R** Response Variety (Shannon entropy of response repertoire)  
- **G** Goal‑Directed Recognition (agency via efference‑copy analogs)  
- **M** Adaptive Modification (learning; e.g., KL divergence updates)  
- **C** Substrate constraints (compute/bandwidth/energetics normalization):contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}:contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}

> **Standardization note.** Earlier drafts showed a summative variant; v3.0 **adopts a multiplicative core** to model synergy among components; divide by **C** enforces substrate fairness:contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}:contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}.

### 2.2 Formal component sketches
- \(D = \sum s_i \cdot \Delta E_i / N\) (signal vs. noise)  
- \(S \approx 1 - H(\text{self‑caused})/H(\text{total input})\) (or boundary overlap)  
- \(R = -\sum P(r)\log_2 P(r)\)  
- \(G \sim 1 - \Delta t_{\text{(action→recognition)}}/\Delta t_{\text{min}}\)  
- \(M = D_{KL}(P_{t+1} \parallel P_t)\):contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}:contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}
